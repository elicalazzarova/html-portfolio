My Favourite Disney movies of all time!

My Top 3

Soul

This movie is for the life after dead. It has very deep meaning and I highly recommned anyone to watch it.

Inside Out

This movies is about the kids and their emotions. It shows how the brain responds to different emotions during different ages. Very useful for both children and parents. A must have one!

Turning Red

This one is about the process of growing up. Really interesting movie that help children in puberty to understand better their emotions and to see this part from the view of parents too.
